ABOUT US

Varidhi International School

Varidhi International School is a top-tier group of schools covering the three major curricula - CBSE, ICSE, and State Board. With a rich history of providing world-class education, a Varidhi International school might just be what your child needs to develop into a leader of tomorrow.

Varidhi International School strives to offer education of the highest quality to help students become the best versions of themselves. We hope to see our students become productive.

  • Why Varidhi International School?

    When it comes to your child’s future, one of the most important decisions you can make is to decide which school to send them to. The choice of school can make a big difference in your child’s life. In this regard, we invite your child to one of our schools. With a rich legacy of providing quality education that spans over 6 years, covering programs in CBSE, ICSE, and State Board. With meticulously selected teachers and wide range of extracurricular activities for students to take part in, you can’t go wrong with Varidhi International School.
