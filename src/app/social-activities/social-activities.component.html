Activities

  1. Makkala Mane Beti (Children’s House visit programmed by Teachers)
  2. Varidhi vathsalya program. (Introduction of tradition and culture of our agriculture, folk arts and Education system)
  3. Vichaara vantharondige varidhi maathukathe. (Discussions with Intellectuals, Media and Academics about the education system and curriculum)
  4. Providing dry ration kits (To the local and village level warriors who works everyday with the Public)
  5. Providing health kits to the public. (For ASHA workers and Anganawadi teachers and Grama Panchayat staff)
  6. Provided stationary to backward students.
  7. Shaala prarambotsava and covid awareness program for Parents and Public
  8. Supported Dalit Literary (Writer) kotigaanahalli Ramaiah during his struggles.
  9. Provided uniforms to the Anthagange buddimaandya makkala shaale. (Uniforms to Mentally challenged children)
  10. We have organised swacha Bharath mission on the hills of therahalli. (Cleaning drive on Plastics)
  11. Women Empowerment, Gender in Development and SHGs /Federations of SHGs
  12. Sexual Harassment, Gender & Domestic Violence / Human Rights.
  13. Moderation and Facilitation Skills – Training of Trainers
  14. Behavioral Change Communication & Management in Development
  15. Curriculum– Modules Development
